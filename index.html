URAC accreditation requires applicants to submit clinical and operational procedures, quality assurance processes, and other organizational information to URAC for review. Generally takes 4-5 months to gain URAC approval as meeting their standards

Once accreditation is granted, every 3 years pharmacies need to reapply and recomplete the whole process to keep this highly esteemed accreditation.
